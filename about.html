About Paniudto

Elevating the local flavors of Cantilan

Our Story

Paniudto was born from a passion to showcase the rich culinary heritage of Cantilan, Surigao del Sur. Our name, derived from the local term meaning "to dine" or "to feast," reflects our commitment to providing not just a meal, but a complete dining experience that celebrates our local culture.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to elevating traditional Cantilan dishes while preserving their authentic flavors. By working closely with local farmers, fishermen, and suppliers, we ensure that every ingredient tells a story of our community's rich maritime and agricultural heritage.

Local Ingredients, Global Standards

Our menu features fresh seafood caught daily from the pristine waters surrounding Cantilan, vegetables grown in the fertile soils of Surigao del Sur, and spices that have been used in local cooking for generations. We believe in sustainable practices and supporting our local economy.

The Team Behind Paniudto

This website is a collaborative project created by IT students who are passionate about both technology and preserving local culture. Our team consists of:

  • Frontend Development: Creating user-friendly interfaces
  • Backend Development: Building robust systems (simulated for this demo)
  • UI/UX Design: Designing intuitive user experiences
  • Content Creation: Researching and presenting local cuisine

Our Vision

We envision Paniudto becoming a bridge between traditional Filipino cuisine and modern dining experiences. Through this platform, we hope to inspire others to explore and appreciate the diverse flavors that our beautiful region has to offer.
