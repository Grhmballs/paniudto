About Paniudto

Elevating the local flavors of Cantilan

Our Story

Paniudto was born from a passion to showcase the rich culinary heritage of Cantilan, Surigao del Sur. Our name, derived from the local term meaning "to dine" or "to feast," reflects our commitment to providing not just a meal, but a complete dining experience that celebrates our local culture.

Our Mission

Paniudto serves the best Filipino dishes in town, offering a unique twist on traditional flavors with a fine dining approach. Each dish is a perfect balance of rich, authentic tastes and creative presentation, showcasing the depth and diversity of Filipino cuisine. The attention to detail, fresh ingredients, and exceptional service make it the top choice for anyone craving elevated Filipino fare.

Local Ingredients, Global Standards

Our menu features fresh seafood caught daily from the pristine waters surrounding Cantilan, vegetables grown in the fertile soils of Surigao del Sur, and spices that have been used in local cooking for generations. We believe in sustainable practices and supporting our local economy.

Our Vision

Located in Cantilan, Surigao del Sur, Paniudto captures the vibrant and distinct flavors of the locality by highlighting the region's fresh, locally sourced ingredients. With an emphasis on seafood from the nearby shores and tropical fruits grown in the area, every dish reflects the authentic tastes of the province.
